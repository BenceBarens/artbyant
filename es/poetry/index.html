The Good Laugh (For Aly)

The good laugh started a few days after we met
when I died,
wheezing with asthma, breathing quiet,
to stop the intrusive thoughts at 4 am from reaching the bed,
cooling down one leg with the fan on max.

Where you made fun of everything about me and all the things I said,
even the way I faced and how I slept
without a pillow, laying down, hitting the mattress bare necked.

And there is not a good laugh I regret
Like rolling on the carpet of the Egyptian themed hotel
cracking up about my Planet of the Apes
mo-cap impression
or my Stressed Out beatbox session.

I guess you could say it's all just good-fun aggression
when you start to question
my sanity a bit.

Because the good laugh hits
late at night, after a cookie's split,
after wine is sipped,
after I slide in the ditch
between the beds that keep us together
until the good laugh decides that enough has been said
and we should puff up the feathers
to hit the hay.

Or you could say
that we have a good laugh every day,
but differentiate
the good laugh as a different stage,
where I know yours as a Southern accent
and you know mine as my strange way
of working words.

Where I might sound like the biggest nerd
explaining what salt water does to your cells
or why the X-Men
movies are out of line.

So I hope we'll live with the good laugh forever
and die of laugher
time after time.

IN EVERY LEAF

En Cada Hoja es el segundo poemario de Antoni. A diferencia de L'Amour-Addict, que se centraba en sonetos, este libro presenta poemas más extensos escritos en verso libre. Los poemas reflexionan sobre el amor, el mundo interior de Antoni y las personas que lo rodean.

La colección está repleta de atmósferas otoñales y pequeños y detallados momentos de la vida de Antoni.

El paquete también incluye un reproductor MP3 con una canción por cada página del libro, creando una banda sonora que se puede escuchar mientras se lee.

L'Amour-Addict

Presentamos L'Amour-Addict, la primera colección de poesía de Antoni van de Geijn, que abre un nuevo capítulo en el universo creativo de Art by Ant. Inspirado en experiencias personales, este paquete explora el amor, el anhelo y la conexión a través de palabras sinceras e imágenes vívidas. Cada poema ofrece una mirada a emociones y momentos reales, fusionando lo personal con lo universal.

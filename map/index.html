Visual universe

Antoni is an artist who transcends traditional abstract painting by creating The Visual Universe - a cinematic world brought to life through art. Rather than producing standalone works, Antoni's paintings function as interconnected scenes from a vast, fictive universe, much like a cinematic universe in film. Each piece is a glimpse into this unfolding reality, capturing moments, atmospheres, and fragments of a larger narrative that exists beyond the frame.
